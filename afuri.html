Ramen Ebisu

Afuri

● ● ○ · avg. ¥1,200

Light, refreshing yuzu shio ramen. A great alternative to heavier styles. The broth has a subtle citrus fragrance and the chashu is tender. The Ebisu location has a nice atmosphere and rarely has a long wait.
