Tonkatsu Meguro

Tonki

● ● ○ · avg. ¥2,000

A Meguro institution since 1939. The counter seats let you watch the precise frying process up close. The rosu-katsu is the classic order. Everything is done with quiet efficiency and the result is a perfectly crisp, juicy cutlet every time.
