Ramen Shinjuku

Fuunji

● ● ○ · avg. ¥1,100

A tsukemen specialist with a rich fish and pork dipping broth. Always has a line but moves quickly. The noodles are thick and chewy, and the broth is deeply savory without being too heavy. Ask for the extra-thick noodles if you like more chew.
